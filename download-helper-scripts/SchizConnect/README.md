# Download instructions 
A quick google search of the fBIRN dataset will lead to a [NITRC page on fBIRN](https://www.nitrc.org/projects/fbirn/). You'll notice, however, that their `Downloads` page doesn't have any information on how and from where to download the imaging data from the study. 

Further investigation into the matter revealed that it's possible to download it from http://schizconnect.org/ in BIDS format! So why doesn't NITRC have this information on their "official" page, you ask? Well, what can I say? Welcome to the club. None of us know. Getting to the point.. 

You'll need to register to download data from schizconnect but if you just want to look around and see what's available for download, you could do without logging in.
